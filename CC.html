Curriculum Connection

Website for PowerSchool University Events

Designed and developed web based course catalog for PSU Events called Curriculum Connection from scratch. This website was designed to train the customers on different PowerSchool products. The effort was to make it intutive for them. This website is responsive across monitor, laptop and tablet devices.

I collected several customer and trainer feedback, to design the website to enhance the user experience. I reconstructed the information architecture, upgraded the site navigation for easy resource access, introduced an intuitive search function, and added 3 new screens to simplify the user experience. Further, I designed an entirely new module to allow the users to personalize the configurations of their devices.

New dashboard design for upcoming event

In preparation for the upcoming 2024 winter event, in November 2023 I took the initiative to completely revamp the website's dashboard screen. This involved a comprehensive redesign aimed at enhancing the user interface and functionality. I made use of Adobe Illustrator to design this new dashboard.

Highlights of the enhancements made:

  • Personalized bookmarks/favorites feature in the CC, enabling users to mark and save preferred courses for quick access.The 'My Favorites' card in the carousel on the dashboard would serve as a convenient hub for accessing these selections.
  • Integration of direct survey links on the website to collect valuable customer feedback and ratings pertaining to their training experiences during PSU events.
  • Incorporating a dedicated section on the website, meticulously designed to share vital announcements, updates, and news relevant to the curriculum and forthcoming PSU events.
  • Addition of a new card in the carousel, facilitating direct access to the PSU Course Selector tool. This tool assists users in selecting courses aligned with their individual interests and requirements for the event, enhancing their overall experience on the CC platform.

Through these strategic enhancements and additions, the revamped dashboard not only elevates the overall customer experience but also contributes significantly to the website's functionality and usability. The development and implementation of this new design is currently in progress.
