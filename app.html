PSU App

End-to-End Creation of the PowerSchool University App
(Design + Development)

As a pivotal part of the PowerSchool University events, I spearheaded the comprehensive design and development of the PowerSchool University(PSU) app from scratch. This project catered to the company's need for an in-house application that offered attendees a centralized platform for accessing event-related information effortlessly.

My objective was to design and develop a offer attendees a seamless user experience by allowing customers to access vital event details such as schedules, training sessions, venue information, and social activities, providing a comprehensive overview of the event's offerings. The app was meticulously designed to put all essential information at their fingertips, ensuring they could navigate and access everything they needed effortlessly.

To materialize this vision, I utilized Adobe XD to craft wireframes and mock-ups, ensuring a user-centric design before diving into the development phase.

Subsequently for development, I brought the app to life using React Native, harnessing its robust capabilities to create a cross-platform solution that guarantees a consistent user experience across devices.

After the development phase, I oversaw the deployment of the app, ensuring a smooth and successful launch onto the App Store. As a result, the app is currently live and available for download, providing seamless access to event-related information for PowerSchool University attendees.
