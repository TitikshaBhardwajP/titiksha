My Wedding Invitation

My Wedding Invitation video


For my wedding, I decided to create the wedding invitation on my own. I first developed the card using Adobe Illustrator and chose the blush pink color for the background to symbolize love; I then added the pink flower for happiness and fragrance. For the itinerary page, I kept the top blue for a calming sky effect, keeping the pink theme at the bottom. The ring at the center of the itinerary page symbolizes a promise of Faith, Hope, and Love. When zoomed in, it can be seen that each ring is made with one of these virtues.


I used Adobe After Effects to add animation and music to it, and added falling flower petals.


To export it as a mp4 file, I made use of Adobe Media Encoder.

Elements


As I was growing up, I always beleived in 3 virtues, which are: Faith, Hope, and Love. And, so I wanted them to be a part of my wedding card. I made a ring of each of these virtues and combined them, to represent a promise. I then, combined our names for the hastag and got "#Niksha". "Nik" from Nikhil, and "ksha" from Titiksha. The word Niksha means kiss in Sanskrit
Created and designed a logo that combines, N(Nikhil) and T(Titiksha) with a heart, using Adobe Illustrator. I then got it cut out on a golden sheet, which was used as the stage background.
